Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new patient?

Click on the "New Patient" button in the Patient Records section and fill in the required information.

Can I schedule recurring appointments?

Yes, you can set up recurring appointments when creating a new appointment by selecting the repeat option.

How do I enable online consultations?

Go to the Online Consult section and enable the availability option, then configure your preferred consultation methods.
